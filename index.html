Our Story

Founded in 2023, The Gentlemen for Export began with a clear mission — to deliver high-quality Egyptian frozen food products to global markets. Starting with premium French fries, we have successfully expanded our exports to the UK, Kuwait, and other international destinations. Today, we’re growing our range to include frozen vegetables and fruits, reflecting our dedication to quality, innovation, and sustainable sourcing. With every shipment, we aim to represent the best of Egyptian production — fresh taste, consistent quality, and reliable service.
