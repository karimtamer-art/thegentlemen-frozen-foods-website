Our Story

From Egyptian Farms to Global Markets

Founded in 2023, TheGentlemen for Export was built on a single conviction: Egypt's agricultural bounty deserves a premium platform on the world stage. We set out to bridge Egyptian farms with international buyers who demand consistency, quality, and reliability.

Today we ship IQF-processed frozen vegetables, fruits, and French fries to buyers in the UK, Kuwait, UAE, Saudi Arabia and beyond — with every shipment backed by cold-chain integrity and export-grade certification.

Our mission is to make Egyptian frozen produce the preferred choice for B2B buyers worldwide — delivering not just a product, but a partnership built on trust.
