Who We Are

Your Global Partner in
Frozen Food Excellence

Founded in 2023, TheGentlemen for Export has grown from an ambitious Egyptian export startup into a trusted international supplier of premium frozen food products. Our commitment to quality, safety, and reliability has earned us the trust of importers and distributors across multiple international markets.

We specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting a wide range of premium frozen foods — including French fries, vegetables, and fruits — sourced from Egypt's finest agricultural regions. Our cold-chain facilities ensure every product maintains peak freshness, nutritional value, and taste.

Our rigorous quality control measures and export logistics capabilities allow us to offer competitive pricing without compromising on the premium standards that define TheGentlemen brand.
